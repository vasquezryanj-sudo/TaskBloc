Privacy Policy Last updated: March 2026
MindWell is a personal reading and writing database. We take your privacy seriously.
What we collect When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address. We store the reading entries, notes, quotes, and annotations you create in the app. We do not collect any other personal data.
How we use it Your data is used solely to power your personal MindWell account. We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or analytics.
Data storage Your data is stored securely via Supabase, a cloud database provider. Each user's data is private and inaccessible to other users through row-level security policies.
Third parties We use Google OAuth for sign-in and Supabase for data storage. No other third-party services have access to your data.
Deletion To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at mindwelladmin@gmail.com.
